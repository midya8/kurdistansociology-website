We are pleased to announce that the **Kurdistan International Sociological Association (KISA)** is now online.

KISA is an independent, scholarly, non-partisan, and not-for-profit academic association dedicated to advancing sociology, social research, teaching, publication, and professional exchange. We bring together sociologists, social researchers, academics, graduate students, public intellectuals, and research-oriented institutions interested in Kurdish society, Kurdistan, the Kurdish diaspora, the wider Middle East, and comparative global sociology.

## What's next

- **First International Conference** — Erbil/Hawler, 2–3 September 2026. The full Call for Papers is now [available on this site](/en/conference).
- **Kurdistan Sociological Review** — our annual peer-reviewed journal will begin accepting submissions soon.
- **Membership** — applications are open. See [Members](/en/members) for details.
