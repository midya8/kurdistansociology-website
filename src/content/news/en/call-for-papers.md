The **Kurdistan International Sociological Association (KISA)** invites paper proposals for its **First International Conference**:

> **Kurdistan in Sociological Perspective: Social Transformation and Global Connections**
>
> 2–3 September 2026 · Erbil / Hawler, Kurdistan Region of Iraq

## Submission

- English abstract of 250–300 words
- Five keywords
- Author's full name, academic title, institutional affiliation, country, and email
- Short biography of approximately 100 words
- Preferred presentation language: English, Sorani Kurdish, or Kurmanji Kurdish

Send submissions to [info@kurdistansociology.com](mailto:info@kurdistansociology.com) with subject line **"KISA 2026 Conference Submission — [Author Last Name]"**.
